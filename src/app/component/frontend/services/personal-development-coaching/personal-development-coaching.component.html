Why do you require Personal Development Coaching? Or do you even need it? Well, as Heraclitus of Ephesus, a pre-Socratic Ionian Greek philosopher, once said, “The Only Constant In Life Is CHANGE!” Everything around you is in a constant state of change. Everything is continually developing and advancing. If you decide not to change at the same time, or do it at a pace slower than that of the things changing around you, you will always find yourself falling back.

In such scenarios, you will most likely find yourself stuck in the same place, watching everything else rush by, or move aimlessly and achieve nothing at all. Life around you will continue to move, evolve, expand or contract, and grow, while you will move nowhere, eventually reducing to dust with no trace left behind. You must continue to learn, develop new talents and skills, and become a better version of yourself than you currently are if you are to leave behind a mark for others to remember you. Even if you do not wish to achieve anything of significance, you still need to evolve in order to survive the testing conditions of daily life.
